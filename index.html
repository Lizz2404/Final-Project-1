About

Hello, it's a pleasure to meet you! To begin, I consider myself an artist at heart. I enjoy drawing, painting, sculpting, and writing in my free time. My artistic journey began in childhood, with my earliest canvases being the walls of my bedroom. From a young age, I always aspired to have a career that allowed me to be creative. I was the kid in kindergarten who said they wanted to be an artist when they grew up. Throughout my young years, I always debated what kind of artist I would become. I thought that one day I was going to become an artist that flew around the world painting murals. Another year, I thought I'd become like Takashi Murakami and create one design that would explode and make millions. Another time, I thought I could become an animator working with the likes of Disney or Studio Ghibli. In today's world, art is everywhere, so finding where and how my artistic abilities would fit was hard, and I'm still figuring it out since the world is constantly changing. But for now, I'm passionate about designing and creating online spaces.

Sketchbook

Welcome to my sketchbook! As mentioned before I love to draw! Infact, I probably have just over 20 different sketches back at home. Although I would love to show them all and my artistic journey through the ages, it would take far too long. This however, is my most recent sketchbook I’m about to finish. I started this sketch book on Monday July 4th 2022. That is not how quickly I usually complete my sketchbooks, but that is what university does to you. I experimented a lot with oil pastels in this sketchbook. I had a lot of fun layering and scratching away at them to create this amazing texture you see on the faces and in the background. I can easily say I like them far more then chalk pastels. Feel free to click on the buttons to change the different views of my sketchbook.

HTML

The following table shows a little about what I have learned about HTML & CSS.


Term Definition
HTML HTML is a standard language for creating web pages. It allows you to structure a web page using elements, heading, paragraph, links, images and more.
Tag Tags are used to mark the beginning and end of an element.
Element An element is similar to a tag but it includes the entire component, this meaning the opening tag, the closing tag and the content in between. For example

"This is a paragraph element"
Attribute An attribute provides any additional information to the browser about the element. They are found in the beginning of a tag of an element and tend to come in name/value pairs like ‘name=”value”.
CSS CSS isn’t the same as HTML but it is how the HTML is stylized. Thus, it is concerned with the layout, colours, fonts and more of the HTML. CSS can directly be included in the HTML or attached in a separate file.
